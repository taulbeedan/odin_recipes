Ham and Cheese Tarts

(24 Tarts)


Description

These savory tarts have been a family favorite for years. Make the ham mixture in advance to save time when guests arrive.


Ingredients

Crust

  • 2 cups "JIFFY" Baking Mix
  • 2/3 cup milk

Filling

  • 6 oz. cream cheese, softened
  • 1 Tbsp. milk
  • 1/4 tsp. dry mustard
  • 3 Tbsp. chives
  • 1/4 tsp.seasoned salt
  • 1/4 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1/2 cup ham, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup sour cream


Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Grease mini muffin pans.
  3. Combine crust ingredients to form a soft dough.
  4. Knead several times on floured surface.
  5. Roll out to 1/8" thickness.
  6. Using a 2 1/2" cutter, cut out 24 biscuits and place in pans.
  7. Mold dough to fit bottom and slightly up sides. Set aside.
  8. For filling, combine cream cheese, milk, mustard, 1 tablespoon of chives, seasoned salt and garlic powder. Blend well.
  9. Fold in ham and sour cream.
  10. Spoon mixture into shells.
  11. Sprinkle reserved chives on top.
  12. Bake 20 - 25 minutes.
