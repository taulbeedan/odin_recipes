Hush Puppies

(24 - 28 Small Hush Puppies)


Description

What better to go with your seafood dinner than some delicious Hush Puppies made with “JIFFY” Corn Muffin Mix.


Ingredients

  • 1 pkg. "JIFFY" Corn Muffin Mix
  • 1 egg
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup onion, finely chopped
  • frying oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oil in deep fryer to 375°F.
  2. Combine muffin mix, egg, milk, flour and onion. Mix well.
  3. Drop by teaspoon into oil.
  4. Fry 4 minutes (2 minutes on each side) or until golden brown.
  5. Drain and serve.
